# CareOS Master Context

## Mission

CareOS is a family care communication platform.

Families do not pay for logs.
Families pay for peace of mind.

The product should help parents, pet owners, and families caring for elders understand what happened during a care session through a warm, human, trustworthy care story.

## Product Positioning

NOT:

* Medical EHR
* CRM
* Task tracker
* Technical logging system

YES:

* Family communication platform
* Care transparency platform
* Trust-building platform
* Storytelling platform for care

## Design Philosophy

Visual direction:

* Blue / green CareOS palette
* Calm
* Trustworthy
* Apple-like simplicity
* Rounded cards
* Soft shadows
* Human language

Avoid:

* Dark themes
* Aggressive colors
* Heavy dashboards
* Overly bold typography

## Supported Care Types

* Child care
* Pet care
* Elder care

## Core User Flow

Caregiver starts session

↓

Adds updates

↓

Shares Moments

↓

Family follows Today's Care Story

↓

AI creates Daily Story

## Main Page

Current center of product:

app/sessions/page.tsx

This is the most important screen in CareOS.

## Current Core Features

### Session Timer

* Start Session
* End Session
* Elapsed timer
* Remaining countdown timer

### Session Actions

* Start Sleep
* End Sleep
* Meal
* Medicine
* Mood
* Activity
* Note
* Moment

### Today's Care Story

Purpose:

Show a human-friendly story of the session.

Not a technical timeline.

### Moments

Purpose:

Allow caregivers to share meaningful updates with families.

Language:

"Share a Moment"

Not:

"Photo Report"

### AI Summary

Future direction:

Human story.

Not a technical report.

Example:

Today Anna spent the afternoon with Willy. Willy enjoyed a walk, ate chicken and rice, took a nap, and stayed happy throughout the day.
